# Prizm - Product Pitch & Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

**Prizm** is a next-generation DeFi lending protocol that democratizes access to real-world asset (RWA) collateral. By bridging institutional-grade finance with blockchain technology, Prizm enables users to earn sustainable yields through transparent, decentralized lending backed by tangible assets.

---

## The Problem

### Current Market Gaps

1. **Yield Crisis**: Traditional finance offers 4-5% APY; DeFi alternatives are risky or volatile
2. **Collateral Mismatch**: Most DeFi relies on crypto collateral (circular risk)
3. **Institutional Distrust**: Cryptocurrency lacks real-world asset backing
4. **Accessibility**: RWA products locked behind high minimums ($100k+)
5. **Verification Complexity**: Manual KYC/AML processes are time-consuming

### Addressable Market

- **TAM**: $348 billion (DeFi lending market cap projected 2025)
- **SAM**: $42 billion (RWA segment growing 45% YoY)
- **SOM**: $500 million (Target users: 10k DAUs by Year 1)

---

## The Solution: Prizm

### Value Proposition

**"Institutional-Grade Yields Through Real-World Assets"**

Prizm offers:
- ✓ **Stable Yields**: 8-15% APY backed by real assets (real estate, trade finance, renewable energy)
- ✓ **Transparent Collateral**: Every loan backed by audited, tokenized assets
- ✓ **Low Barriers**: Accessible starting at $100 (minimum investment)
- ✓ **Instant Verification**: 4-step onboarding in < 5 minutes
- ✓ **User-Centric Design**: Beautiful, intuitive interface with zero friction

### Product Architecture

**Three Core Components:**

1. **Asset Pools** (Supply Side)
   - Real Estate mortgages
   - Trade finance invoices
   - Supply chain finance
   - Renewable energy projects

2. **Lending Markets** (Demand Side)
   - Dynamic interest rates
   - Collateral-based borrowing
   - Risk-adjusted pricing
   - Liquidation protection

3. **User Dashboard** (Experience Layer)
   - Real-time portfolio tracking
   - Health factor monitoring
   - Yield optimization
   - Transaction history

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Protocol Fees** (15% of spread)
   - Borrower origination fee: 0.5%
   - Platform management: 1% annually

2. **Institutional Services** (Custom pricing)
   - Vault creation: $50k-500k
   - Tokenization: 0.5-2% of AUM
   - Integration API: $10k-100k annually

3. **Ecosystem Services** (Future)
   - Insurance premiums: 0.1-0.5% of deposits
   - Advanced analytics: SaaS model

### Unit Economics (Projected Year 1)

- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: $45 (organic + referral)
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: $2,400 (4% of yield average 3-year hold)
- **LTV/CAC Ratio**: 53.3x (extremely healthy)
